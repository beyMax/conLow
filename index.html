Kenison, Dudley & Crawford, LLC

Kenison, Dudley, & Crawford, LLC is a boutique law firm which concentrates its practice in construction, business, and aviation matters. It was formed in 2000, and practices in SC, NC, and GA. With offices in Greenville, Columbia, and Atlanta, the attorneys can either take primary responsibility for matters, or act as local counsel.
The firm handles all types of construction matters including representation of Owners, Sureties, General Contractors, Subcontractors, Suppliers and Design Professionals in public and private projects of all sizes and all phases including negotiation and drafting of documents, liens, bond claims, and litigation. Its business practice includes formation of entities, partner disputes, dissolutions, and general and complex commercial litigation. The firm's aviation practice includes representation of airmen and mechanics in enforcement actions by the FAA, purchase and lease transactions, accident litigation, and insurance litigation.
